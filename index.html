CSCW 2024 Workshop

Positech: Technology “Beyond Fixes” and Towards Human Flourishing

Sunday, October 15, 2024, 10am-5pm (TBU)
Workshop Proposal

Overview

The current focus of social technology research, largely on mitigating negative impacts of social media, often misses the potential of these technologies to enhance human connections and well-being. With social media's integral role in daily life, there's a crucial need to shift towards a holistic view of its impact on human flourishing.

This workshop is organized around three themes to address this: 1) "Exploring Relevant and Adjacent Research" to define and widen the Positech scope with insights from related fields, 2) "Drawing the Landscape of PosiTech" for participants to outline the domain’s key aspects and 3) "Envisioning the Future of Positech," anchored around strategic planning towards a sustainable research community. Ultimately, this workshop aims to broaden the social technology research narrative towards a more positive, human-centric approach, encouraging exploration that prioritizes human well-being and fosters meaningful connections.

Call for Participation

→ Submission Link

We will invite 25-30 individuals interested in the positive use of technology for human flourishing to participate in our hybrid-format workshop. Submissions are welcomed in several forms:

  • Position papers or drafts (2-6 pages, ACM single-column format, excluding references) discussing themes of the workshop.

  • "Encore" submissions of relevant conference or journal papers.

  • Statements of research interest (up to 2 pages, ACM single-column format, excluding references).

Workshop Agenda

Reimagining social technologies through a lens that prioritizes increasing positivity necessitates a collective effort beyond merely altering research focuses individually. Our workshop aims to bring together researchers focused on expanding the scope of social technology research with an emphasis on positivity and human flourishing.

Time Activity and Description
10:00-10:15am Welcome and Introduction: Opening remarks from the organizers to introduce the workshop's goals and structure.
10:15-10:30am Coffee Break
10:30-11:15am Theme 1: Panel Discussion: Selected panel leads discussion on the intersection of social technologies and human flourishing, setting the stage for the day's activities.
11:15-12:00pm Author Presentation: Authors who volunteered prior to the workshop will briefly introduce their work. After the short presentation (3 - 4 min), each participant will have a short Q&A session from the audience (2 - 3 min).
12:00-1:20pm Lunch Break
1:20-2:30pm Theme 2: Breakout Sessions: Small groups work on drafting syllabi for a hypothetical Positech course.
2:30-3:00pm Theme 2: Synthesizing Syllabi: Attempt to integrate the various group syllabi into a cohesive curriculum, time permitting.
3:00-3:15pm Coffee Break
3:30-4:00pm Theme 3: Group Discussion: Brainstorming solutions for sustained engagement for the Positech research community.
4:00-4:50pm Workshop Report: Summarizing the day's outcomes and outlining next steps for action and collaboration.
4:50-5:00pm Endnote: Concluding remarks from the organizers.
6:30pm (Optional) Dinner Social Event
